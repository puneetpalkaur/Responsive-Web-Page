Night of 1000 Jack O' Lanterns


More than a 1000 illuminated Jack O' Lanterns were carved by extraordinary artists and put on display at the Governor's Island, New York City. The adventure begun with a Ferry ride from Governor Island Ferry Harbor in Manhattan, offering a lit view of Manhattan skyline , Brooklyn Bridge and Statue of Liberty on its way. The trail on Governor Island was light up with thousands of meticulously hand painted and hand- carved pumpkins. From Disney to Game of Thrones, several major characters were carved intricately with grace.
